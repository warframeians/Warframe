Overview

Warframe is an Online Cooperative Sci-Fi Shooter game developed by Digital Extremes. The story takes place in an alternate universe, similar to our own but far in the future. You play as a "Warframe", a flexible metal suit of armor created by the Orokin; An empty husk, controlled remotely by the your Tenno operator.
